# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 15, 2026

Bliksem Studios ("we", "us", or "our") built JUSPlay as a free, open-source application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

## Information We Collect

JUSPlay connects to **your own self-hosted Subsonic-compatible music server**. We do not operate any server infrastructure and do not collect, store, or transmit any of your data.

### Data stored on your device

- **Server credentials** (URL, username, password) — stored locally on your device to connect to your server. These are never transmitted to us or any third party.
- **Playback preferences** (streaming quality, theme, cache settings) — stored locally on your device.
- **Cached music and artwork** — temporarily stored on your device for offline playback.

### Optional: Gemini API Key

If you choose to use the AI-powered smart playlist feature, you may provide your own Google Gemini API key. This key is stored locally on your device and is used solely to communicate with the Google Gemini API. We do not have access to this key.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect personal information
- We do not collect usage analytics or telemetry
- We do not collect crash reports
- We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
- We do not share any data with third parties

## Third-Party Services

JUSPlay communicates only with:

1. **Your Subsonic server** — the server you configure in the app settings
2. **Google Gemini API** (optional) — only if you enable the smart playlist feature and provide your own API key

We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the privacy practices of your Subsonic server or Google's Gemini API. We encourage you to review Google's privacy policy if you use the smart playlist feature.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/BliksemStudios/JUSplay/issues).
